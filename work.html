Ford Built Homes

Overview:
The client construction company wanted to give the business an updated online presence. It would feature a homes the company has built as well as some pictures of the insides of the homes. The team rebranded his logo and built the theme of the site around the new logo colors.
My Contribution:
The orginal project was canceled due to communication issues during developement. I then used the created assessets to finish developement on my own using the developer tool Webflow. I put together the final layout of the website's landing page as well as built the gallery. The website is fully responsive to function on all screens including desktop, tablet, and phone.
